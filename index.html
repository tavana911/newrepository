How I Work

THE
PROCESS

Systems thinking powers both disciplines. Whether I'm shipping code or framing a shot, the underlying logic is identical: understand deeply, plan precisely, execute with intention, then refine until it's right.

⟨/⟩
DEVELOPMENT PROCESS
01
Discovery
Deep dive into business goals, user needs, and technical constraints. Asking the right questions before writing a single line.
02
Architecture
Planning component structure, state management, and information flow. The blueprint before the build.
03
Build
Clean, semantic code. Iterative development with regular checkpoints. Responsiveness and accessibility baked in from the start.
04
Optimize
Performance audits, accessibility checks, cross-browser testing. Nothing ships slow.
05
Deploy
Confident deployment via GitHub, version-controlled and documented. Handoff with clarity, not confusion.
⬛
FILM PRODUCTION PROCESS
01
Concept
Translating the brief into a visual idea. What is the emotional core? What should the audience feel at frame one — and at the last cut?
02
Pre-Production
Storyboarding, shot lists, location scouting, and lighting design. Every creative decision made before the camera rolls.
03
Shoot
Deliberate camera operation, intentional lighting, and controlled composition. Capturing coverage that enables powerful editing.
04
Edit
Building the sequence in Premiere Pro. Pacing, rhythm, and narrative arc refined until every cut serves the story.
05
Color, Sound & Delivery
Grading for emotional consistency. Layering audio for immersion. Final export optimized for the intended platform.
